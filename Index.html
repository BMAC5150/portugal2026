🇵🇹 Eating in Portugal

Spring in the Algarve brings a viticultural renaissance and seasonal abundance. Carvoeiro's culinary soul is rooted in its fishing village heritage — charcoal-grilled seafood, cataplana copper-pot stews, and petiscos (Portuguese tapas) shared over local wine. In Lisbon's Castelo and Alfama, centuries of tradition meet modern creativity, all within walking distance of your apartment on Rua de Santa Cruz do Castelo.
